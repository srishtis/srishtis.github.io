Projects

This section covers all the projects that I have completed in the field of data sciences and analytics. The projects belong to mainly 3 buckets: Undergrad university project, Kaggle projects and descriptions of professional projects I have completed in my career as a decision scientist.

Gender classification of Speech Signals using ANFIS

Features like pitch, amplitude and entropy of the speech signals were extracted to create an ANFIS based classification model for gender recognition of the speaker.

Price Elasticity of automotive aftermarket parts

Due to lack of sufficient price-demand points for given automotive parts, these products were clustered on the basis of their functional, physical and market-performance attributes. These clusters were then subjected to a generalized linear model (log-log) to establish the price-demand elasticity curve and equation.

Emerging Issue Analytics- Vehicle Recall Prediction

Customer complaints from NHTSA were treated and cleaned with text processing methods: Stop Word removal, bigram treatment, stemming, word-frequency treatment, word-length treatment. These treated complaint phrases were then analyzed using STM (structural topic modelling) to derive key themes and vehicle failure patterns. Using alternative data sources like warranty data and customer transaction data, a vehicle recall was predicted 3 years(on an average when tested for 2 historical test cases) in advance.

New Product recommendation- UK Retail

Based on attributes and market performance of competitor products, new products were identified to include in the client portfolio. The performance (impact of introduction) of these parts were predicted on the basis of historical performance of similar products in the clients' product portfolio.

Identify claims that are related to a catastrophic event that were misclassified as non-catastrophic claims.

Insurance claims were analyzed for 3 aspects: Keywords in claim descriptions(term frequency), temporal and geographical attributes. These attributes were used to create multiple classification models and then the best performing model was selected to differentiate catastrophe-induced claims from the non-catastrophic claims.

Machine Failure Prediction(Predictive Maintenance)

For an injection molding machine, 2 different buckets of features were selected: performance atributes and maintenance features. Additional features were engineered to create multiple classification models to predict machine failure an hour in advance.

Kaggle- Kickstarter Project performance prediction

Built a classification model to predict the success of a Kickstarter project using historical Kickstarter projects data with attributes like name of the project, duration, category, goal and other derived metrics. I also published an articles on my Medium profile giving a walkthrough of the process and results.

Pricing Toolkit for automotive parts

Built an RShiny dashboard for the aftermarket sector of a leading automotive giant: Reporting Module: automated reports spare-parts pricing anomalies; Pricing Post-Audit Module: conduct test-control analysis for identifying the revenue, margin and volume impact of recent pricing decisions; Strategic Pricing Module: several heuristic and algorithmic approaches to prescribe directional price recommendations on automotive spare-parts

Analytics Vidhya- Prediction of time taken to solve a programming problem given a users' current status

This multiclass classification problem uses a problem and a programmers' attributes to predict range of attempts it will take the programmer to solve the problem. The description of this problem statement states that the outputs of the model will serve as an input to a recommendation engine that will help the programming committee suggest relevant problems to the participants to solve and provide hints automatically on which they can get stuck.

Kaggle- Prediction of House Prices

Using over 75 predictors, a regression model was created after extensive data exploration, feature engineering and data treatment. The results are currently in the top 31% of the leaderboard.
